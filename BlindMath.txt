
BlindMath: blindmath@nfbnet.org

This group is a place where interested persons can discuss all issues
related to blindness and math. Topics for discussion include (but
are not limited to) sources for accessible texts, information about
tactile and auditory graphing programs, suggestions for insuring that
math lectures  are accessible to blind students, and strategies used
by blind math  instructors. Anyone with an interest in this topic is
free to join  this group and contribute  to the discussions.

This list is moderated by Steve Jacobson. You can reach him at:
steve.jacobson@visi.com

See also http://www.blindscience.org/blindmath-gems-home
